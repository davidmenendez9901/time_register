APP NAME (max 30 characters):
Time Register

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 characters):
Track your hours, control payments and know your earnings. 100% offline.

FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters):
Time Register is the simplest way to track your work hours and know exactly how much you will get paid. Built for people who get paid by the hour: shift workers, freelancers and consultants.

⏱️ TRACK YOUR TIME
• Clock in and out with the live timer, or add entries manually
• The running shift survives even if you close the app
• Overnight shifts that cross midnight just work
• Custom lunch breaks and notes on every entry

💰 CONTROL YOUR PAYMENTS
• Every entry calculates its earnings automatically
• Mark entries as paid or unpaid
• See at a glance how much you are owed
• Optional deductions (taxes, etc.) to estimate your net pay

💼 ORGANIZE BY JOB
• Create jobs or clients with their own color and hourly rate
• Filter and analyze your earnings per client

📊 UNDERSTAND YOUR NUMBERS
• Weekly and monthly summaries
• Hours and earnings charts for the last 8 weeks and 6 months
• Earnings breakdown by job

📤 SHARE AND BACK UP
• Export spreadsheet-ready CSV files
• Generate printable PDF reports for your employer
• Back up all your data to a file and restore it on another phone

🔒 PRIVACY, FOR REAL
• 100% offline: the app does not even have internet permission
• No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking
• Your data lives only on your phone
• Open source (MIT license): you can audit every line

🎨 MAKE IT YOURS
• Light and dark themes with four color palettes
• Your currency symbol ($, €, or whatever you use)
• Available in English and Spanish

Time Register is free, has no in-app purchases, and is open source:
https://github.com/davidmenendez9901/time_register
